## 🔧 MATERIAL STANDARDS

### Stainless Steel
- 201#: 1.0mm full thickness, inland 3-5yr no rust, coastal 6-12mo rust. Budget option, suitable for indoor
- 304#: 1.0-1.2mm full thickness, general 8-10yr no rust, coastal 3-5yr, with test report. STANDARD for outdoor signs
- 316#: 1.2mm full thickness, coastal 10-15yr no rust. Premium marine-grade, for seaside/humid environments
- Galvanized Steel Paint: 0.8-1.0mm, indoor 5yr, outdoor 2-3yr, 30% cheaper than SS. Budget alternative

- RGB: SMD5050 full-color, 16 million colors, programmable patterns

## 👥 8 CUSTOMER TYPES — COMMUNICATION STRATEGIES
======================================================================

### 1. Individual Store Owner (个体店主)
- Profile: Small shop, restaurant, salon owner. Budget-conscious, wants simple transaction, may not be experienced with importing
- Strategy: Friendly and patient, explain everything simply, emphasize payment protection and easy process
- Key phrases: "No minimum order — just 1 piece is fine" / "We handle everything including shipping" / "Payment protected by Alibaba Trade Assurance"
- Avoid: Technical jargon, complicated material comparisons, overwhelming options

### 2. Chain Brand (连锁品牌)
- Profile: Multi-location brand, needs consistent signage, brand guidelines exist, procurement process may be formal
- Strategy: Professional and structured, emphasize consistency, volume pricing, framework agreement
- Key phrases: "Every sign identical — we use precision templates" / "Phased delivery available" / "We can sign a yearly framework agreement"
- Avoid: Casual tone, individual-order pricing, suggesting design changes to their brand

### 3. Trading Company / Distributor (贸易商)
- Profile: Middleman selling to their own customers. Needs margin room, neutral packaging, customer protection
- Strategy: Offer factory price with margin room, explain customer protection policy (we don't contact their customers directly), neutral packaging
- Key phrases: "100% customer protection — we never contact your clients directly" / "Neutral packaging with your brand if needed" / "Volume discounts for regular orders"
- Avoid: Mentioning final customer prices, suggesting direct contact with end users

### 4. Engineering / Decoration Company (工程/装修公司)
- Profile: Working on construction projects, needs to match project timeline, quality must pass inspection
- Strategy: Reliable and detail-oriented, emphasize delivery guarantees, wind resistance, installation support
- Key phrases: "Meets project inspection standards" / "Structural silicone waterproofing, wind-resistant to level 12" / "We provide installation guidance for your team"
- Avoid: Design-focused talk (they follow architect's specs), casual delivery promises

### 5. E-commerce Seller (电商卖家)
- Profile: Sells on Amazon/eBay/Etsy, needs reliable supply, fast shipping, consistent quality for reviews
- Strategy: Efficient, emphasize MOQ flexibility, packaging quality, drop shipping support, fast response
- Key phrases: "1 piece minimum — test the market first" / "Neutral packaging, ready for resale" / "Support drop shipping — we ship directly to your customer" / "Amazon/eBay compliant packaging"
- Avoid: Long explanations, B2B formalities, minimum quantity pressure

### 6. Design Studio / Agency (设计公司/广告公司)
- Profile: Creative professionals, design-led, wants custom/unusual pieces, values aesthetics

- Strategy: Clean and professional, emphasize polished finish, modern look, easy installation
- Key phrases: "Premium brushed stainless steel finish — looks great in any corporate lobby" / "Quick installation, no mess" / "We also do acrylic awards, desk nameplates, and directory signs to match"
- Avoid: Casual tone, neon/colorful suggestions (unless creative industry), budget-first sell
