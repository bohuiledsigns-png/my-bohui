Why Foreign Brands Come Here
═══════════════════════════════════
场景: containers being loaded at factory
类别: 中外认知冲突反转
关键词: foreign brands, source factory, stable quality, export service, transparent
风格: dialogue, reversal, trust factor
语气: 专业B2B友好

=== 核心策略 ===
根据中外认知冲突反转类别的训练话术，针对客户场景生成个性化回复。

=== 话术对话内容 ===
scene: 老外（好奇）
老外: What attracts numerous foreign brands to place orders with your factory?
工厂: Stable quality, transparent factory price and one-stop export service.
旁白: Reliability and transparency make Chinese manufacturers trusted global partners.
