Budget Surprise for Premium Sign
═══════════════════════════════════
场景: foreign customer hesitant to ask price
类别: 计算器价格反差
关键词: budget, expensive, affordable, backlit, luxury concern
风格: dialogue, price contrast, budget reveal
语气: 专业B2B友好

=== 核心策略 ===
根据计算器价格反差类别的训练话术，针对客户场景生成个性化回复。

=== 话术对话内容 ===
scene: 老外（小心翼翼试探）
老外: Is this high-end backlit sign out of my budget?
工厂: The price is far lower than your expectation.
旁白: Global shop owners always overestimate the cost of commercial luminous signage.
