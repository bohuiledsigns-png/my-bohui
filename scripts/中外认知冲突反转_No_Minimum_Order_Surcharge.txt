No Minimum Order Surcharge
═══════════════════════════════════
场景: small boutique storefront
类别: 中外认知冲突反转
关键词: small order, single store, no surcharge, long-term cooperation
风格: dialogue, reversal, flexible policy
语气: 专业B2B友好

=== 核心策略 ===
根据中外认知冲突反转类别的训练话术，针对客户场景生成个性化回复。

=== 话术对话内容 ===
scene: 老外（意外）
老外: You also accept tiny single store custom orders without extra surcharge?
工厂: We don't set order quantity threshold, focus on long-term stable cooperation.
旁白: Other suppliers reject small orders or add hidden surcharges arbitrarily.
