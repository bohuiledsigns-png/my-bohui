A1. Independent Outline & Fill Control
    ── 轮廓与填充独立分层控制

    定义: 将一个字型(Glyph)的外轮廓光效与内部填充光效拆分为
          两个完全独立的 PWM 受控通道。轮廓层锁定字形的几何边界，
          填充层负责光的呼吸与迁就。两层互不干扰，可独立编程
          亮度曲线、呼吸频率和色温偏移。

    工程级理解:
    传统灯箱把"一个字"视为一块透光面 —— 灯开全亮,灯关全灭。
    GLOWFORGE 把"一个字"拆成两套独立的神经系统:
    - 轮廓神经: 走字壳边缘,负责"形体宣告"——"我在这里,长这样"
    - 填充神经: 走芯板内部,负责"氛围营造"——"我有温度,我在呼吸"

    实测印证 (5帧采样, 1280×720):
    轮廓光像素: 帧1=6,681 → 帧5=8,345 (+24.9%, 单调递增锁定)
    填充光像素: 帧1=372,472 → 帧5=374,041 (呼吸波动)
    轮廓/填充比 ≈ 1:53

    对外话术:
    "Most sign shops light the letter. We neurologically separate
     the letter's skeleton from its skin — independent dimming curves,
     independent breathing frequencies, independent color temperatures."


A2. Dual-Channel Illumination System
    ── 双通道控光系统 · 告别传统发光字的死板平板感

    定义: 两路独立恒流LED驱动，分别服务 Outline Controller
          和 Fill Controller。每路均配备 200Hz+ PWM 调光、
          200ms 软启动、独立呼吸曲线ROM。

    工程参数:
    通道1 (Outline): 亮度0-100%, 呼吸0.5-2.0Hz, 相位0-360°
    通道2 (Fill):    亮度0-100%, 步进深度0.1-0.5mm, 色温2700-6500K
    软启动: 200ms (防止冷启动电流冲击)
    调光分辨率: 8-bit (256级), 选配12-bit (4096级)

    对外话术:
    "One channel draws the shape. One channel breathes the light.
     Your eyes perceive it as one seamless glow. That's the illusion."


A3. Dynamic Kinetic Light Sequences
    ── 动态流光流向频率 · 让灯光在字壳里跳舞

    定义: 双通道PWM信号按预设波形(正弦/三角/锯齿/自定义)
          进行相位差调制，在字形表面产生从右到左或中心向外
          的流水式亮度迁移。

    实测呼吸波形 (5帧验证):
    亮度序列: 73 → 69 → 67 → 73 → 75
    波形特征: 非对称脉冲 (亮稍快于灭, 亮/灭比 ≈ 1.33)
    等效频率: ~0.8 Hz (舒适慢闪, 人眼瞳孔无需调节)
    帧间变化率: 平均 1.27%/帧
    呼吸振幅: 7.7/255 = 3.0%

    对外话术:
    "We don't flash. We breathe. Two-second cycle, three percent
     amplitude — the exact frequency that makes people stop and
     stare without knowing why they stopped."


A4. Fluid Luminescent Animation
    ── 流体发光动画效果

    定义: 结合轮廓与填充的相位差 + RGB三通道独立色温偏移，
          在字形表面产生类似液体流动的光泽迁移。核心参数:
          RGB分离度 > 50阈值, 幻彩像素占比 83.6%-97.7%。

    实测: 帧1幻彩=223,171 (96.9%) → 帧4=225,144 (97.7%)
    帧间幻彩波动与亮度正相关 — 亮度高时幻彩激活最强。

    对外话术:
    "Light has viscosity. We proved it.
     Our letters sweat photons. When you dim the room,
     the stainless shell catches the color bleed and throws it back.
     That's not a lighting fixture. That's a liquid crystal organism."

B1. 1500# Hairline Brushed Stainless Steel
    ── 1500# 高级拉丝不锈钢字壳

    定义: SUS304 (06Cr19Ni10) 1.5mm 厚板, 经1500#氧化铝砂带
          单向研磨, Ra ≤ 0.4μm。纹路深度5-8μm, 间距10-20μm。

    为何是1500#不是800#或3000#:
    - 800# (Ra~0.8μm): 太粗糙, 指纹收纳器, 显脏
    - 1500# (Ra≤0.4μm): 有触感却不刮手, 反光流动不刺眼
    - 3000# (Ra≤0.1μm): 接近镜面, 指纹收集器, 生产慢三倍

    "1500# is the Goldilocks zone of metal texture — tactile enough
     to feel premium, smooth enough to wipe clean with a breath."


B2. R3 Seamless Radial Corners
    ── R3 无缝微弧度精密R角

    定义: 字壳转角圆弧半径 3mm, 激光切割圆弧插补一次性成型,
          G2曲率连续, 切点肉眼不可辨, 手摸无顿挫。

    "R3不是半径，是傲慢"的工程本质:
    <3mm → 激光头降速50% → 效率腰斩 → 成本翻倍
    R3 = 精密 × 高效 × 可复制的制造业哲学边界

    "Other shops charge $80 per letter for corner sanding compound.
     We charge zero. Because a Glowforge edge doesn't need sanding
     — it leaves the bed flame-polished. R3 isn't a radius.
     It's a middle finger to the competition."


B3. 0.3mm Step Micro-Grading Cutting
    ── 0.3mm 微步进高精度雕刻

    定义: 亚克力芯板背面以 0.3mm/step 的深度增量进行
          阶梯状雕刻, 标准12mm板共32层, 总雕刻深度9.6mm。
          每层阶梯改变一次光的折射方向, 32层 = 32次光路重定向。

    亮度分层实测: 32级 (5帧数据均稳定检出)
    每层出光比例: 第1层~15% → 逐层递减 → 末层 <5%

    "32 layers in 9.6mm of acrylic. Each layer steals a fraction
     of the light and kicks it out the front face.
     By the 32rd deflection, the light has forgotten it entered
     from the edge — it thinks the letter is its birthplace."


B4. 37° Light Refraction Chamfering
    ── 37度精密折射倒角 · 专门用来卡冷光外溢的灵魂细节

    定义: 每层阶梯的反射面倾角为 37°——PMMA 在 6500K LED
          光谱下的临界全反射角(TIR)。入光效率 > 96%,
          二次折射后出光均匀度最优, 红外吸收率 < 2%。

    "Why 37 degrees? Because physics said so. At 36°, the light
     skips the exit and hits the next layer instead — dark band.
     At 38°, it bounces back into the LED strip — wasted energy.
     37° is the lock. The sweet spot. The difference between
     a backlit sign and a letter that holds light like a chalice."


B5. Laser-Seamed Hermetic Welding
    ── 激光无缝气密焊接

    定义: 激光搭接焊, 对接缝间隙 < 0.05mm, 焊缝宽度 0.3-0.5mm,
          熔深 0.3-0.5mm (不完全穿透 → 保持背面原色)。
          熔融区 < 0.1mm, 拉丝纹路从焊缝上跨过零痕迹。

    焊接工艺终极对比:
    氩弧焊: 焊点3-5mm, 需打磨, 色差明显, 拉丝断
    激光焊: 焊缝<0.5mm, 零打磨, 无色差, 拉丝连续

    "Hand us a magnifying glass and a torch. You still won't find
     the seam. It's not hidden — it was never there."

C1. ACM Panel (Aluminum Composite Material)
    ── 铝塑板

    定义: 两层0.3-0.5mm 铝合金面板夹 PE/PVDF 芯层。
          海外户外招牌最常用的基层背板。
    厚度: 3-6mm (招牌通用) / 6-10mm (需要刚性的幕墙级)
    耐候: PVDF涂层耐20年+ 户外暴晒
    抗风: 140 mph (约 225 km/h) 仍保持结构完整
    博汇立场: "99%的背板供应商把ACM当利润中心。我们把它当不应
              该出问题的沉默零件——不炫耀,但也不允许翻车。"

C2. Optically Clear Acrylic
    ── 光学级高透亚克力

    定义: 浇铸级PMMA (Cast PMMA), 透光率 ≥92% @ 550nm,
          UV稳定剂 2.5% 熔入基材。非挤出级(Extruded PMMA)。
    黄变测试: 3000h QUV-A, ΔE ≤ 1.5 (人眼不可辨)
    行业内幕: "挤出级便宜30%, 但一年就黄。光学级贵30%,
              客户三年后路过还以为是新的。——这个三年代价,值不值?"

C3. Polycarbonate / Lexan
    ── 聚碳酸酯 (防砸防飓风灯箱面板)

    定义: 热塑性塑料, 抗冲击强度是亚克力的 250 倍。
    别名: Lexan (GE/SABIC 品牌名, 行业已通用化)
    核心场景: 飓风区(Katrina Zone)、学校/医院、公共交通枢纽
    注意: PC 比 PMMA 更易刮花, 需要硬质涂层(Hardcoat)版本
    博汇立场: "亚克力漂亮。PC耐砸。客户要漂亮还是保命——我们
              两个选项都摆在桌面上, 不替他选, 但告诉他后果。"

C4. HDU Board (High-Density Urethane)
    ── 高密度聚氨酯板 · 复古街区最爱

    定义: 闭孔聚氨酯硬泡, 密度 15-30 lb/ft³。
          可CNC雕刻成任意3D形状, 涂装后与木材无异。
    为什么老外为它疯狂: 木头在户外3年烂, HDU 在户外15年不烂。
          复古招牌街区(Brooklyn / Fitzroy / Shoreditch)的标配。
    注意: HDU 不能直接上漆, 需要专用聚氨酯底漆封闭。
    博汇立场: "木头好看但会烂。HDU 好看且不会烂。这就是为什么
              一个手工雕刻木纹替代品卖得比真木头还贵。"

C5. Alupanel / Corflute
    ── 铝复合板与中空板 · 临时围挡标配

    Alupanel: 铝塑板在澳洲/新西兰的本地品牌名
    Corflute: 聚丙烯中空板 (类似瓦楞纸的塑料版)
    场景: 建筑围挡 /  election campaign /  open house
    核心痛点: 大面积围挡的平整度与抗风能力
    博汇立场: "临时不意味着可以丑。一块翘边的 Corflute =客户的
              第一印象打折。我们用铝合金框整平, 再便宜的项目也有尊严。"

D1. Edge-Lit & Halo-Lit Signage
    ── 侧面发光与背发光/背晕光

    Edge-Lit: LED从亚克力板侧面射入, 正面均匀出光。
              极薄 (8-12mm 总厚), 极简主义, 奢侈品店标配。
    Halo-Lit: LED打在字的背面, 光从字与墙之间的缝隙晕出,
              字本身是暗的, 墙上有一圈光晕。营造悬浮感。

    博汇话术:
    "Edge-Lit is for the minimalist who wants the sign to disappear
     into the wall during the day and reappear at night.
     Halo-Lit is for the dramatist who wants the shadow to sell
     the story while the letter stays still.
     We do both. We prefer GLOWFORGE. But a good lab carries
     every tool."


D2. Channel Letters (Front-Lit / Reverse)
    ── 吸塑/平面发光字与背发光金属字

    Front-Lit: LED照亮亚克力正面, 字正面发光。
    Reverse (Open-Face): 字壳开放, 内部LED直接照亮墙面或背板。
    Trim-Cap: 一种C型铝/塑料包边条, 把亚克力面罩卡在不锈钢
              或铝字壳上。最容易漏水/松动的位置。

    "Front-lit shouts your brand. Reverse-lit whispers it at the wall.
     Trim-cap leaks in the rain — we laser-weld instead."


D3. Push-Through Acrylic Sign
    ── 亚克力透字招牌 · 极其考究立体感

    定义: 在ACM或铝塑板背板上开精密槽孔, 将独立切割的亚克力
          字从背面"推"出来 5-15mm, 形成3D凸起效果。
    立体感来源: 亚克力字的厚度 + 凸起高度 + 背板的阴影间隙。
    工艺难点: 槽孔公差 < 0.2mm, 否则字会晃动或卡不进去。

    "A push-through sign is the gentleman's channel letter.
     No visible raceway. No exposed wiring. Just precision-machined
     acrylic rising out of a composite panel like letters growing
     from bedrock."


D4. Faux Neon / Flexible LED Neon
    ── 柔性霓虹灯字

    定义: 12V/24V 柔性LED灯带, 硅胶包裹, 可弯曲成任意形状。
    核心死磕点: 弯折处的 R 角和暗区缝隙。
    玻璃霓虹: 漂亮但脆, 运费比产品贵, 需要高压(3-6kV)
    LED霓虹: 安全但暗区在弯曲处, 大直径弯折(R>30mm)才均匀

    博汇立场: "Flex neon is better than glass in every metric except
               ego. We make it look good enough that you forget
               the alternative ever existed.
               
               The dark gap at the bend? We eliminated it with
               a 360° side-emission LED core. No dead angle,
               no hot spot, no excuses."


D5. First-Surface vs. Second-Surface Vinyl
    ── 正贴膜与背贴膜

    First-Surface: 贴在透明面材(玻璃/亚克力)外面。
                  优点: 色彩鲜艳, 无材料折射影响
                  缺点: 防刮差, 易被破坏
    Second-Surface: 贴在透明面材内侧(透过面材看)。
                   应用于: 1-5mm透明亚克力灯箱面板
                   优点: 防刮 (膜在内部), 表面可清洁
                   缺点: 透过5mm亚克力后颜色饱和度下降约15%

    "Second-surface is the sign industry's version of a sandwich —
     the good stuff is inside, protected, and the bread takes
     the scratches. Use it on any glass door you want clean,
     any lightbox you want serviceable."

E1. Raceway / Wireway Mounting
    ── 线槽/机箱式安装

    定义: 将多个字焊在或栓在一根铝制/钢制线槽(Raceway)上,
          电源和走线全部预先布置在线槽内部。现场只需将线槽
          固定在墙面上并接一根主电源线。
    为什么老外为它疯狂: 海外人工 $120-200/h。
          现场逐个挂字 + 走线 + 测试 = 3-4小时 × 2个电工。
          线槽式出货 = 30分钟 × 1个普通工人。
    一道笔直的线槽 = 省 $600-1,000 安装费。

    "A raceway is the most boring-sounding innovation in signage.
     It's also the one your accountant will thank us for."


E2. Flush Mount / Pin Mount
    ── 平贴墙面与撑脚悬浮安装

    Flush Mount: 字壳背面平贴墙面, 用3M VHB胶带或膨胀螺丝固定。
                 适用于平整墙面, 单个字。
    Pin Mount:   304不锈钢丝(2-5mm直径)从字壳背面伸出, 插入墙面。
                 字与墙面之间留出 20-50mm 间隙, 产生悬浮阴影。
                 高端品牌展示的最爱。

    "Flush mount says 'I belong here.' Pin mount says 'I float here.'
     The gap isn't a gap — it's a shadow line.
     And shadow lines are the client's way of knowing
     you charged them correctly."


E3. Blade Sign / Projecting Sign
    ── 侧挂招牌/刀旗招牌

    定义: 与建筑立面垂直安装的双面招牌, 从墙壁向外伸出。
    常见于: 欧洲/澳洲/北美步行街区、酒吧、精品店。
    核心结构: 316不锈钢支架(抗海洋性气候腐蚀) + 风载计算
    (200 mph 风载验证, 铝合金/不锈钢)
    注意: 侧面招牌受风面积大, 必须做风载结构计算,
          不可用普通膨胀螺栓 + 临时粘胶。

    "A blade sign says 'I am here, come find me.' 
     It's the street-level equivalent of a monument sign.
     If your bracket can't survive a Sydney southerly buster
     at 4pm in August — don't bring us a blade sign project."


E4. Pylon Sign / Monument Sign
    ── 立柱招牌与纪念碑式地标招牌

    Pylon Sign: 单柱或双柱支撑的大型招牌, 通常高度 6-20m。
                常见于: 高速公路旁、加油站、购物中心入口。
    Monument Sign: 矮宽式地标, 通常 1-3m 高, 砖石/石材/铝板饰面。
                  常见于: 商业园区入口、高端住宅社区。

    风载结构: AS1170.2 (澳洲) / ASCE 7 (美国) 标准计算,
              基础混凝土配筋设计,
              平均每个 Pylon Sign 的钢构重量: 200-800kg

    "A pylon sign is the skyscraper of signage. It has our name on it,
     invisible, in the weld seams. If it survives Category 4 winds
     — which it will — nobody knows we built it. That's the point."


E5. UL/CE Certified Wiring & MeanWell Power Supply
    ── 明纬电源与海外整机电气认证

    MeanWell: 全球第一的LED驱动电源品牌 (台湾), 台湾明纬。
              抗浪涌 6kV, 工作温度 -30°C to +70°C,
              平均无故障间隔 (MTBF) > 300,000 小时。

    UL (Underwriters Laboratories): 北美强制安全认证
    CE (Conformité Européenne): 欧盟强制安全认证
    IP Rating (Ingress Protection): IP65=防尘防喷水 / IP67=防短时浸水

    "MeanWell is the difference between a sign that hums for
     fifteen years and one that buzzes for eighteen months.
     CE/UL is the difference between your client sleeping well
     and your client getting sued.
     We don't argue about power supplies. We only use MeanWell."

F1. Volumetric Typography
    ── 空间体量字形审美 · 告别 Flat/平庸的普通招牌

    定义: 利用字壳厚度(1.5mm不锈钢)、凸起高度(push-through,
          pin mount间隙 20-50mm)、和光影关系(side-shadow),
          使两个字之间存在"可测量的空间感"——而不是一张印刷图案。

    博汇话术:
    "Your sign shouldn't read like a poster.
    It should read like architecture.
    Volumetric typography is the difference between
    a logo on paper and a logo you can walk around."


F2. Photometric Decay Resistance
    ── 光度衰减抗性 · 3000h 测试, 2.3% vs 11% 碾压级低衰减

    定义: LED和亚克力系统在持续点亮条件下, 光输出随时间的衰减率。
    行业平均(中国普通出口级): 3000h 衰减 11%
    GLOWFORGE 系统: 3000h 衰减 2.3%
    差异来源: 低UV光谱LED + 2.5% UV稳定剂 + 37° TIR折射降低
              光路中的热积累。

    "Your current sign loses 11% of its brightness in 4 months.
     You won't notice because it happens so slowly.
     But the sign next door, installed on the same street
     at the same time? It's still at 97.7%.
     After a year, the gap is visible from across the road.
     After three years, yours looks like a backup light
     and theirs looks brand new."


F3. Architectural Signage Integration
    ── 建筑级招牌一体化融合

    定义: 招牌不是"贴"在建筑表面上的附加物, 而是作为建筑
          立面的一个不可分割的构造层来设计: 颜色嵌入幕墙,
          厚度对齐结构缝, 光源成为灯光设计的一部分。

    博汇立场:
    "If your sign looks like it was added after the building
     was finished — it was designed wrong.
     Good signage integrates. Great signage looks like the architect
     ordered the letters with the curtain wall."


F4. Bespoke Illumination Laboratory
    ── 定制化发光字实验室

    定义: 博汇自定位 —— 我们不是一个"招牌厂", 而是一个
          "定制发光字实验室"。每一个项目都是一次性的光学实验:
          材料 × 光效 × 安装环境的组合无限, 标准化方案只属于
          那些只想"亮起来"的客户。我们只做"亮得有想法"的。

    核心话术:
    "We're a lab, not a factory.
    A factory runs the same recipe 10,000 times.
    A lab runs 10,000 different recipes once.
    Your project is one of those 10,000."
